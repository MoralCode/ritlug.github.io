Events Committee

The events committee organizes club activities.

    Give a Talk

    Do you think that something is awesome and would like to share it? If you have a topic that you would like to present on, bring it up to eboard and we can make arrangements to have you present.
    Help us at Events

    Each year we attend events such as: the Rochester Maker Faire, Imagine RIT and Club fairs. During these events we demo club and member projects. If you are interested in presenting one of your projects or just helping us, keep an eye on our email lists for a call for volunteers.

Projects Committee

The project committee fosters club projects by providing infrastructure and other resources.

    Join a Project

    RITlug has tons of cool and interesting projects such as TigerOS and Teleirc that you can work on.
    Start a Project

    If you have a cool project idea or already have a personal project, you can bring it into the club to enlarge its community and share it with others.
