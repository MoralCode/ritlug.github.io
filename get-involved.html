Meetings are open to anyone interested. RITlug meets on every {{ site.ritlug-day }}, {{ site.ritlug-time }} in {{ site.ritlug-place }}.

If you can't make the whole time, that's fine! Meetings typically have a presentation first, then we open the floor to discussion and technical help. We also hold "Project Collaboration" sessions where you can come and work on projects, homework, or just hang out. Interested? Just show up!

Download Events Calendar

Give a Talk

Do you think that something is awesome and would like to share it? Each week we have a presentation on a topic relating to something Open Source. If you have a topic that you would like to present on, bring it up to eboard and we can make arrangements to have you present.

Learn More

Work on a Project

RITlug has tons of cool and interesting projects such as TigerOS and Teleirc that you can work on. If you have a cool project idea or already have a personal project, you can bring it into the club to enlarge its community and share it with others.

View our GitHub

Join us at Events

Each year we attend events such as: the Rochester Maker Faire, Imagine RIT and Club fairs. During these events we demo club and member projects. If you are interested in presenting one of your projects or just helping us, keep an eye on our email lists for a call for volunteers.
